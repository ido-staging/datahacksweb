What is DataHack?

DataHack 2015 is the first data-oriented hackathon in Israel, designed to bring together the Israeli data community.
The hackathon is a 48-hour event where data-enthusiastic programmers, designers and researchers combine their skills to build innovative solutions for real-world problems.
Registration is open to all, but applications will be carefully reviewed for skills and experience. Participants will have complete freedom selecting data, tools, and project ideas.

Who are we?

We are a group of Computer Science M.Sc. students from The Hebrew University of Jerusalem, specializing in areas such as machine learning, data science, bioinformatics and brain science, passionate about all things data.
We have partnered with MadeInJLM and Siftech, which will contribute their experience organizing tech events -- including hackathons -- in Jerusalem, and with experienced individuals from both academia and industry, to help us create an amazing event.
