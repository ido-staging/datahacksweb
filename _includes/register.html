Who can register?

Any data scientist, programmer, designer, statistician, researcher - any person who loves data. You can be a student, an industry professional, someone from academia. We don’t care. It’s best to come with at least some experience in programming, data science, design. However, you do need to be at least 18.

Forming teams

Coming to hackathons after already forming a team with people you love working it is a great idea, but if you don't have a team we will help you find one! Our registration process incroporates HakkaLabs matching mechanism to help you find other participants with complementary skill sets to form a team with. Teams of up to five members are allowed.
